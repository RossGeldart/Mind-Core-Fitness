FAQ

Got Questions?

Do I need equipment?
Nope. Core Buddy works with whatever you have — bodyweight, dumbbells, kettlebells, or a full gym. The randomiser adapts to your available equipment.
How long are the workouts?
You choose. Workouts range from 5 to 30 minutes. Perfect for busy schedules — even a 5-minute session counts.
Is it really free to start?
Yes. The free plan gives you 2 workouts per week with 5 and 10 minute options. No credit card required. Upgrade whenever you want.
Can I cancel anytime?
Absolutely. Cancel from your account settings at any time. No penalties, no hassle. Your free trial won't be charged if you cancel before it ends.
Does it work offline?
Yes. Core Buddy is a Progressive Web App that works offline. Complete workouts without an internet connection — your data syncs when you're back online.
What devices does it work on?
Any device with a browser. iPhone, Android, tablet, laptop, desktop. Install it to your home screen and it works just like a native app.
Is my data private?
100%. Your data is stored securely and is private by default. We don't sell your information to third parties.
Do I need to download from the App Store?
No. Core Buddy is a web app — just visit the site in your browser and add it to your home screen. No app store, no downloads, no storage space wasted.
