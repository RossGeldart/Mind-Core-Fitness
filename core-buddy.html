FAQ

Questions? We've Got Answers.

Do I need equipment?

Nope! Core Buddy works with whatever you have: bodyweight, dumbbells, barbells, or a full gym. The randomiser adapts to your equipment.

Can I do a quick 5-minute workout?

Absolutely! Core Buddy offers workouts from 5-30 minutes. Perfect for busy days when you just need to move.

How do leaderboards work?

Compete with your friends and groups on weekly workout counts, consistency streaks, and challenges. Friendly competition that keeps you motivated.

Can I keep my progress private?

Yes! Your social feed can be set to private (only you see it) or public (share with buddies). You control what you share.

Can I use it offline?

Yes! Core Buddy is a Progressive Web App (PWA) that works offline. Complete workouts and log data without internet.

What if I can't hit 3-4 workouts per week?

That's okay! Adjust your goal to 2-3. Core Buddy adapts to YOUR reality. The point is consistency at whatever level works for you.

Do I have to take progress photos?

Nope! Progress photos are optional. Some people love them, some don't. Use the features that work for you.

What badges can I earn?

Earn badges for consistency streaks, total workouts, challenges completed, helping buddies, and more. Each achievement celebrates your progress.

Is my data private?

100%. Your photos, measurements, and stats are private by default. You control what (if anything) you share with buddies.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. Cancel anytime from your account settings. No penalties. No hassle.
