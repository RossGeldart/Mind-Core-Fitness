Programme Questions

What equipment do I need?

90% of the programme is bodyweight. Some weights like a dumbbell and kettlebell are introduced later in the programme.

How long are the workouts?

Workouts range from 12–20 minutes. Short, focused sessions that fit into any schedule.

What if I'm a complete beginner?

The Core Foundation phase is designed specifically for beginners. You'll learn proper form and build up progressively. No prior experience needed.

Do I get lifetime access?

Yes. Once purchased, the programme is yours forever. Repeat it as many times as you like.

Absolutely not! I work with all fitness levels, from complete beginners to experienced athletes. Everyone starts somewhere.

What should I wear or bring?

Just comfortable workout clothes and trainers. Water and towels are provided at the studio.

24 hours notice required for cancellations. Life happens — just let me know as soon as you can.
