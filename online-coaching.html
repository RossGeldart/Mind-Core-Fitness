FAQ

Common Questions

What equipment do I need?

Most programmes can be done with minimal equipment. Specific requirements are listed with each programme, but bodyweight options are always included.

How do I access my programme?

Download the Everfit app (free on iOS and Android), and your programme will be delivered directly to you after purchase.

Can I message Ross for help?

Yes! All programmes include direct messaging through the Everfit app. Ask questions, send form check videos, and get real support.

What level should I start at?

If you're new to core training, start with Core Foundation. If you've been training consistently, Intermediate or the full 12-Week programme is ideal.

How long do I have access?

You get lifetime access to any programme you purchase. Train at your own pace and revisit whenever you want.

Why join programmes on Everfit?

Everfit gives you personalised workout plans, progress tracking, and access to a community of like-minded individuals - all in one professional platform.
