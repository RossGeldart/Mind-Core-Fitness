Frequently Asked Questions

What equipment do I need?

90% of the programme is bodyweight. Some weights like a dumbbell and kettlebell are introduced later in the programme.

How long are the workouts?

Workouts range from 12-20 minutes. Short, focused sessions that fit into any schedule.

What if I'm a complete beginner?

Phase 1 starts from the ground up. If you can hold a basic plank, you're ready to begin.

Can I message Ross for help?

Yes! Direct messaging through the Everfit app is included. Ask questions, send form check videos, get real support.

How do I access the programme?

After purchase, you'll receive an invite to the Everfit app where all your workouts and tracking are managed.

How long is the programme?

12 weeks of structured training across three progressive phases. Complete at your own pace.
