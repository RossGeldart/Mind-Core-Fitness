Mind Core Fitness — Core Buddy App

If you would like to delete your Core Buddy account and all associated data, please follow the steps below.

How to Request Account Deletion

Send an email to ross@mindcorefitness.com with the subject line "Account Deletion Request" and include the email address associated with your account.

We will process your request within 7 days and confirm once your account has been deleted.

Request Account Deletion

What Data Will Be Deleted

Upon account deletion, the following data will be permanently removed:

  • Your account profile (name, email address)
  • Workout history and saved workouts
  • Nutrition and food logs
  • Habit tracking data and streaks
  • Body stats and measurements
  • Buddy connections, posts, comments, and likes
  • Push notification tokens and preferences

Data That May Be Retained

Certain data may be retained where required by law or for legitimate business purposes:

  • Payment and transaction records (as required by financial regulations)
  • Data necessary to comply with legal obligations

Questions?

If you have any questions about the deletion process, please contact us at ross@mindcorefitness.com.

You can also review our full Privacy Policy for more information about how we handle your data.
